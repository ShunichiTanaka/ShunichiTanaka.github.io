Privacy Policy - mizu:log

Last updated: December 3, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application "mizu:log" (the "App") handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information:

1-1. Information provided by users

  • Text information such as plant names, notes, and area names
  • Watering history (dates, notes, etc.)
  • Plant photos (stored locally or taken via the camera)

Important:

All user data is stored locally on your device (e.g., using local databases such as Hive). This information is never transmitted to or stored on external servers.

1-2. Information collected by third-party services (Advertising)

The App uses Google AdMob provided by Google LLC to display advertisements. Google may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS)
  • IP address, device information (OS version, device model, language settings, etc.)
  • Ad interaction data (such as impressions and clicks)

This information is not used to personally identify users and is used for ad personalization and performance measurement.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Ads Policy

2. Purpose of Use

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  1. To provide app functionality (plant management and watering reminders)
  2. To improve app quality and fix issues
  3. To deliver and optimize advertisements

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

However, as described above, third-party services such as Google may automatically collect certain technical information for advertising purposes. We may also disclose information if required by law.

4. Data Retention

Data stored within the App remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.

5. User Control

5-1. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time using the in-app deletion features.

5-2. Ad Tracking

You can disable ad tracking (IDFA) through your device settings.

  • Settings: Settings App > Privacy & Security > Tracking

6. Disclaimer

The App is intended to assist with plant care. The developer is not responsible for any damages, including plant loss, caused by missed notifications, app errors, or data loss.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
