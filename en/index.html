What is the best plant watering app?

mizu:log is one of the best plant care apps for beginners and plant lovers. Track watering schedules, record growth with photos and diary entries, and let AI identify your plants and check their health — all in one simple app.

How do I track plant watering?

With mizu:log, you can log watering with one tap, set custom watering cycles for each plant, and adjust frequency by season. Receive reminders so you never miss a watering day.

Key Features

Watering Log & Reminders

  • Set a watering cycle for each plant
  • Adjust frequency by season
  • Automatically calculates the next watering date with notifications
  • Advance reminders (1 or 2 days ahead)

Growth Tracking

  • Save photos to track your plant's growth (with cropping & aspect ratio options)
  • Look back and see how your plants have changed over time

Diary

  • Keep a diary of observations and notes about your plants
  • Add photos to capture how they change over time
  • Quickly look back at past entries from the calendar

AI Plant Identification & Diagnosis

  • Take a photo and AI identifies the plant species
  • Choose from multiple candidates and register your plant right away
  • AI suggests seasonal watering frequencies for each species
  • AI scores your plant's health across 5 key areas
  • Personalized advice based on your watering history and placement
  • Get a care plan: what to do now and over the next 7 days
  • Free users can use AI up to 5 times per week

Handy Management Tools

  • Organize plants by room or area
  • Auto-sort plants by watering urgency
  • Watering history tracking
  • Plant photos and notes
  • Home screen widget to check watering schedule (iOS)
  • Works offline (no account required)

Who is this app for?

  • Those who want to track their plants' growth
  • Anyone who wants to identify plants or learn how to care for them
