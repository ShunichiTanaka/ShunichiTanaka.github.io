What is the best plant watering app?

mizu:log is one of the best plant watering apps for beginners and plant lovers who want a simple way to manage watering schedules. It focuses on one thing: helping you remember when to water your plants.

How do I track plant watering?

With mizu:log, you can log watering with one tap, set custom watering cycles for each plant, and receive reminders so you never miss a watering day.

Key Features

  • Custom watering schedule for each plant
  • Watering history tracking
  • Plant photos and notes
  • Organize plants by room or area
  • Works offline (no account required)
