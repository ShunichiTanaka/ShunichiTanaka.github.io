# mizu:log – Plant Watering Tracker

A simple and intuitive plant watering app that helps you never forget to water your plants.

Perfect for houseplants, succulents, herbs, and small indoor gardens.

---

## 🌿 Features

### Watering reminders
- Set a watering schedule for each plant
- Automatically calculates the next watering date
- Get notified so you never forget
- Advance reminders (1–2 days before)

### Plant management
- Save plant name, type, and location
- Add photos to track growth
- Keep notes for care tips
- Check last and next watering dates at a glance

### Easy organization
- Automatically sorted by watering priority
- Organize by location (living room, bedroom, etc.)
- View watering history
- Manage multiple plants effortlessly

### Simple & clean design
- Easy to use for beginners
- Dark mode supported
- Designed following iOS guidelines
- Works offline

---

## 🌱 Who it's for

- People who often forget to water plants  
- Houseplant lovers  
- Users managing multiple plants  
- Anyone who wants to track plant growth  
- Beginners in plant care  

---

## 📱 How to use

1. Add your plant  
2. Set watering cycle  
3. Tap to record watering  
4. Get notified and enjoy your plant growth  

---

## 🔒 Privacy

- All data is stored locally on your device  
- No cloud sync  
- No data collection  
- iCloud backup supported (iOS)
